## 3. HOW YOU EARN

### 3.1 Commission Structure

As an Affiliate, you earn a commission on qualifying purchases made by individuals you refer to the LifePreneur Platform.

**Current Commission Rate:** 50% of the net purchase amount for each qualifying new subscription.

A **"qualifying purchase"** means:
- A new subscription initiated by a person who has not previously subscribed to LifePreneur
- The purchase is completed within the referral attribution window (see Section 3.2)
- The purchase is not subsequently refunded, reversed, or charged back
- The purchase is not made by the Affiliate themselves (self-referral is prohibited)

### 3.2 Referral Attribution Window

**Cookie Duration:** 30 days from the date a user first clicks your referral link.

If a referred user completes a qualifying purchase within 30 days of clicking your unique referral link, the commission is attributed to you. If the purchase is made after 30 days from the initial click, no commission is earned for that referral.

### 3.3 What Does NOT Qualify for Commission

The following are **not** eligible for commission:
- Self-referrals — purchasing through your own affiliate link
- Purchases made after the 30-day referral window has expired
- Purchases that are refunded, reversed, or charged back after the commission is credited
- Referrals generated through prohibited promotional methods (see Section 5)
- Duplicate, fraudulent, or manipulated referrals
- Purchases by individuals already registered in our system

### 3.4 Commission Rate Changes

**Commission rates are set at the Company's sole discretion and may be modified at any time.** We will provide active Affiliates with at least **30 days written notice** of any reduction to the commission rate. Notice will be sent to the email address on file with your account.

Changes will not affect commissions already earned on qualifying purchases completed prior to the change's effective date.
