## 1. OVERVIEW

This Refund Policy explains how refunds are handled for purchases made through the LifePreneur Platform ("Platform"), offered by LifePreneur, LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our").

## 2. REFUND ELIGIBILITY

### 2.1 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a **7-day money-back guarantee** from the date of your **initial subscription purchase**.

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- Request must be submitted **within 7 days** of your first payment
- Applies only to your **first subscription payment** (monthly or yearly)
- Account must be in good standing (no Terms violations)

**The 7-day period begins:**
- On the date your payment is successfully processed by Stripe
- You will receive a purchase confirmation email with the purchase date

### 2.2 What is NOT Eligible for Refund

**The following are NOT eligible for refund:**
- **Subscription renewals** (monthly or yearly auto-renewals)
- **Requests after 7 days** from initial purchase date
- **Second or subsequent payments** (after your first billing cycle)
- **Partial refunds** for unused time within a billing period
- **Accounts terminated** for Terms and Conditions violations
- **Duplicate purchases** made in error (contact support for resolution)
- **Chargebacks** initiated without contacting us first

**Why No Refunds on Renewals?**

Renewals are automatic recurring charges that you authorize when subscribing. You can cancel your subscription at any time to stop future renewals (see Section 6).

### 2.3 Subscription Type Specifics

**Monthly Subscriptions:**
- 7-day refund window applies to the first payment only
- Subsequent monthly renewals are not refundable
- Cancel anytime to prevent future charges

**Annual Subscriptions:**
- 7-day refund window applies to the first payment only
- If refunded, you receive a full refund of the amount paid (minus payment processing fees)
- No partial refunds for unused months within the year
- Cancel anytime to prevent renewal at the end of the year

### 2.4 Additional Digital Products

One-time digital products, courses, or resources ("Additional Products") available for purchase within or alongside the Platform are **not eligible for refunds**. By purchasing any Additional Product, you acknowledge that delivery occurs immediately upon payment and that no refund will be issued except where required by applicable law.

If a refund for an Additional Product is granted at our sole discretion as an exception, access to the product will be immediately revoked.

## 3. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

### 3.1 Submission Process

To request a refund within the 7-day window:

**Step 1:** Email **support@lifepreneur.com** with subject line "Refund Request"

**Step 2:** Include the following information:
- Your full name
- Email address used at checkout
- Date of purchase
- Subscription type (monthly or yearly)
- Reason for refund (optional but helpful)

**Step 3:** Use the **same email address** you used to create your account

### 3.2 Response Time

- We aim to respond to all refund requests within **2-3 business days**
- Refund processing may take an additional **5-7 business days** after approval
- You will receive email confirmation when the refund is processed

### 3.3 Verification

To protect your account security:
- We may ask verification questions to confirm your identity
- We may require confirmation that you authorized the refund request
- Refunds cannot be processed by unauthorized third parties

## 4. REFUND EVALUATION & APPROVAL

### 4.1 Discretionary Review

**Refunds are granted at the sole discretion of LifePreneur, LLC.**

We strive to be **fair, flexible, and reasonable** when evaluating refund requests. Each request is reviewed individually, and we consider factors such as:

- Whether the request is within the 7-day window
- Your level of engagement with the Program
- Whether Terms and Conditions were violated
- Previous refund history
- Circumstances of the request

**Submitting a refund request does NOT guarantee approval.** However, we approach each request with understanding and flexibility.

### 4.2 Reasons We May Deny a Refund

We may deny refund requests if:
- **Outside 7-day window:** Request submitted more than 7 days after purchase
- **Not first payment:** Request for renewal payment or subsequent charge
- **Terms violations:** Account violated our Terms and Conditions or Community Guidelines
- **Abuse or fraud:** Evidence of fraudulent activity or abuse of the refund policy
- **Intellectual property misuse:** Downloaded, copied, or distributed Program materials
- **Chargeback initiated:** You filed a chargeback before contacting us
- **Previous refunds:** Multiple refund requests across different accounts (abuse pattern)

### 4.3 Approval Notification

If your refund is approved:
- You will receive email confirmation of approval
- The refund will be processed within 5-7 business days
- Funds will be returned to your original payment method
- Your access to the Program will be immediately revoked (see Section 5)

### 4.4 Denial Notification

If your refund is denied:
- You will receive an email explaining the reason
- Your subscription will remain active
- You can still cancel your subscription to prevent future charges (no refund for current period)

## 5. ACCESS AFTER REFUND

### 5.1 Immediate Revocation

**If your refund is approved, your access is immediately revoked.** This includes:

- **Platform Access:** Your account is deactivated and you cannot log in
- **Discord Community:** You are removed from the private Discord server

- **Affiliate Program:** Affiliate account is terminated (if applicable)

**There is no grace period.** Once a refund is processed, access ends immediately.

### 5.2 Cancellation vs. Refund

**Important Distinction:**

| Action | Money Returned? | Access Duration | Future Charges? |
|--------|----------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Refund (within 7 days)** | ✅ Yes (full refund) | ❌ Immediate revocation | ❌ Cancelled automatically |
| **Cancellation (no refund)** | ❌ No | ✅ Until end of billing period | ❌ No future charges |

**Example 1 (Monthly, refund):**
- You subscribe on Jan 1
- You request a refund on Jan 5 (within 7 days)
- Refund approved → You get your first payment back, lose access immediately

**Example 2 (Annual, refund):**
- You subscribe on Jan 1 for an annual plan
- You request a refund on Jan 6 (within 7 days)
- Refund approved → You get your full annual payment back, lose access immediately

**Example 3 (Cancellation, no refund):**
- You subscribed on Jan 1
- On Jan 20, you cancel (outside 7-day window)
- No refund, but you keep access until Feb 1 (end of billing period)

## 7. PAYMENT PROCESSING FEES

### 7.1 Stripe Processing Fees

All payments are processed through **Stripe**, our third-party payment processor.

**Stripe charges non-refundable processing fees:**
- Approximately 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (varies by card type and country)
- These fees are paid to Stripe, not retained by us
- Stripe may not refund their processing fees when we issue a refund

**What this means for you:**
- If you paid $99, Stripe charged us approximately $3.18 in fees
- When we refund you $99, we may not recover those fees from Stripe
- You receive the full $99 refund; we absorb the processing fees

### 7.2 Currency Conversion & International Fees

If you paid in a currency other than USD:
- Your bank may charge currency conversion fees
- These fees are charged by your bank, not by us
- We cannot refund fees charged by your bank or payment provider
- International transaction fees are non-refundable

**Refunds are processed in the original currency and amount you paid.**

### 7.3 Impact on Refund Amount

**Good News:** You receive a **full refund** of the amount you paid to us, even though we lose money on processing fees.

- Monthly subscription: Full $99 refund
- Yearly subscription: Full $999 refund

**We absorb processing fees as part of our 7-day money-back guarantee.**

**Before initiating a chargeback with your bank or credit card company, please contact us first** at support@lifepreneur.com.

**If you have a legitimate dispute**, contact us first. We will work with you to resolve it fairly.

### 9.4 Fraudulent Chargebacks

**Filing a fraudulent chargeback** (claiming you didn't receive the service when you did) may result in:

## 10. NO REFUNDS AFTER CANCELLATION

### 10.1 Cancellation Policy

You may **cancel your subscription at any time** to stop future billing.

**When you cancel (outside the 7-day refund window):**
- Future charges are stopped immediately
- You retain access until the end of your current billing period
- No refund is provided for the current period
- You can re-subscribe at any time

**Example:**
- You subscribed on January 1
- On January 20, you cancel (outside 7-day window)
- You are NOT charged on February 1
- You keep access until February 1 (end of paid period)
- No refund for January (you paid for the month, you get the month)

### 10.2 Partial Refunds Not Available

**We do NOT offer partial refunds** for unused time within a billing period.

**Why no partial refunds?**
- Digital content is accessed immediately and cannot be "un-accessed"
- Our costs (hosting, infrastructure, Discord) are fixed regardless of your usage
- Proration would be administratively burdensome and economically unfeasible
- Subscription pricing assumes ongoing access, not pay-per-use

**Fair Policy:** Cancel anytime, keep access for what you paid for.

## 14. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Can I get a refund after 7 days?

**A:** No, refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of your initial purchase. After 7 days, you can cancel your subscription to stop future charges, but no refund is provided for the current billing period.

### Q: What if I cancel on day 6 but my refund isn't processed until day 10?

**A:** As long as you **submit your refund request** within 7 days, you're eligible. Processing time does not affect eligibility.

### Q: Do you refund subscription renewals?

**A:** No. Only your **first payment** is eligible for refund. Renewals are automatic recurring charges that you authorized when subscribing. You can cancel anytime to prevent future renewals.

### Q: What happens if I cancel without requesting a refund?

**A:** You stop future billing but keep access until the end of your current paid period. No money is refunded. See Section 10 for details.

### Q: Can I get a partial refund for unused months on a yearly plan?

**A:** No. We do not offer partial refunds. If you're within 7 days of your initial purchase, you can get a full refund. Otherwise, cancel to prevent next year's renewal.

### Q: What if I was charged twice by mistake?

**A:** Contact support@lifepreneur.com immediately. Duplicate charges are errors, not refunds, and will be resolved promptly.

### Q: Will you refund me if I don't like the Platform?

**A:** Within the 7-day window, we evaluate refund requests with flexibility. If you're not satisfied, submit a refund request and explain why. We'll review it individually.

### Q: Can I get a refund on an Additional Product (one-time digital purchase)?

**A:** Additional Products are not eligible for refunds as they are delivered immediately upon purchase. Exceptions are not guaranteed and are handled at our sole discretion.

### Q: What if I violated the Terms but want a refund anyway?

**A:** Refunds are typically denied for Terms violations. However, we review each case individually. Contact us to discuss.

### Q: How long does it take to receive my refund?

**A:** Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval. Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear in your account.

### Q: Can I rejoin after getting a refund?

**A:** Possibly, but it's at our discretion. Patterns of abuse (subscribing, consuming content, refunding, repeating) may result in permanent bans.
