## 1. OVERVIEW

This Cookie Policy explains how LifePreneur, LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and tracking technologies on our platform at lifepreneur.com.

## 2. WHAT COOKIES ARE

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve user experience, remember preferences, and support essential business operations.

## 3. COOKIES WE USE

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

**Required for platform functionality. These cannot be disabled.**

#### Authentication & Security
- **Session Token** (`better-auth.session_token`): Maintains your logged-in state securely
  - Duration: 30 days
  - Type: HttpOnly, Secure (in production)
  - Purpose: Required to access your account and use the platform

- **CSRF Protection Tokens** (`csrf-token`, `csrf-token-read`): Protect against cross-site request forgery attacks
  - Duration: 24 hours
  - Purpose: Critical security protection for all form submissions and data updates

#### Functional Cookies
- **Language Preference** (`NEXT_LOCALE`): Remembers your selected language
  - Duration: Persistent
  - Purpose: Provide content in your preferred language

- **Cookie Consent** (`consent`): Stores your cookie preference choice
  - Duration: 30 days
  - Purpose: Remember your privacy preferences

#### Affiliate Attribution Cookies
- **Rewardful Tracking**: Attributes purchases to content creators who referred you
  - Cookie Names:
    - `rewardful_alias` - Stores the creator's identifier
    - `rewardful_referral` - Stores your unique referral ID
    - `rewardful.referral` - Full referral data (JSON format)
  - Duration: Set by Rewardful campaign settings (typically 60 days from initial referral)
  - Domain: `.lifepreneur.com` (works across all subdomains)
  - Same-Site: `Lax` (secure for HTTPS)
  - What it stores: Anonymous referral code (e.g., "kyle"), unique referral ID, timestamp
  - Purpose: When you visit via a creator's referral link (e.g., `?via=kyle`), we store cookies to ensure they receive fair compensation if you subscribe
  - Data collected: Referral source only - no personal information or browsing behavior
  - Why essential: Required for our legitimate business interest in fairly compensating content creators. This is not used for user profiling, cross-site tracking, or targeted advertising.
  - Third-party service: [Rewardful Privacy Policy](https://getrewardful.com/privacy)

**Legal Basis (GDPR):** Necessary for contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)) and legitimate business interests (Article 6(1)(f))

### Optional Cookies (Require Your Consent)

#### Analytics Cookies
- **Vercel Analytics**: Privacy-friendly anonymous usage statistics
  - What it does: Measures page views, performance metrics, and general traffic patterns
  - What it stores: **No browser cookies** - uses server-side tracking only
  - Data collected: Page URLs, referrer, browser type, geographic region (country/city level)
  - No personal data: Does not collect IP addresses, user identifiers, or cross-site tracking data
  - Privacy-friendly: Cannot be used to identify individual users
  - Default: Disabled until you consent
  - Third-party service: [Vercel Analytics Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/docs/analytics/privacy-policy)

**Legal Basis (GDPR):** Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) - can be withdrawn at any time

## 4. HOW WE USE COOKIES

Cookies and tracking tools help us:

- **Provide secure access** to your account and protect against unauthorized access
- **Remember your preferences** such as language selection
- **Fairly compensate creators** who refer new members through our affiliate program
- **Improve the platform** by understanding which features are most valuable (with your consent)
- **Protect security** and prevent fraud through CSRF protection
- **Deliver the service** you've subscribed to access

## 5. MANAGING YOUR COOKIE PREFERENCES

**"Essential Only":**
- ✅ Authentication & security cookies (always required)
- ✅ Functional cookies (language, consent)
- ✅ Affiliate attribution (business operations)
- ❌ Analytics cookies (blocked)

**"Accept All":**
- ✅ All essential and functional cookies

### Changing Your Mind

You can update your preferences at any time by:
1. Clearing your browser cookies (you'll see the banner again)
2. Contacting us at support@lifepreneur.com to reset your preferences

### Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:
- Block all cookies
- Delete existing cookies
- Receive notifications before accepting cookies
- Set preferences for specific websites

**Important:** Disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging in and using the platform.

## 6. DO NOT TRACK (DNT) SIGNALS

Our website does not currently respond to Do Not Track browser signals due to the lack of industry-wide standards and unified technical specifications for DNT implementation.

## 7. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT VIA COOKIES

### Essential Cookies Collect:
- Session identifiers (random tokens, no personal data)
- Authentication status
- Language preferences
- Referral source (when you click an affiliate link)
- CSRF tokens for security

### Optional Analytics Collect (With Consent):
- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Browser type and version
- Device type (desktop/mobile/tablet)
- Geographic region (country/city, not specific location)
- Traffic source (how you found us)

### We Do NOT Collect:
- Personal identification information via cookies
- Precise geolocation data
- Cross-site browsing behavior
- Email addresses or names via cookies
- Sensitive personal data

## 8. DATA RETENTION

| Cookie Type | Retention Period |
|-------------|------------------|
| Session cookies | Until logout or 30 days |
| CSRF tokens | 24 hours |
| Language preference | Until manually changed |
| Consent cookie | 30 days (then you choose again) |
