## 7. EARNINGS & INCOME CLAIMS

### 7.1 Earnings Screenshots

When testimonials include earnings screenshots showing:
- TikTok Shop commissions
- Affiliate program earnings
- Product sales revenue
- Account balances or payment receipts
- Income statements or reports

**Important Clarifications:**

**Gross Income ≠ Net Profit:**
- Screenshots typically show revenue, not profit after expenses
- They do not account for: product costs, advertising spend, equipment, software, taxes, refunds, or time investment
- Net profit may be significantly lower (or negative) compared to gross revenue shown

**Point-in-Time Snapshots:**
- Screenshots represent earnings at a specific moment in time
- They may show peak performance, not consistent or average earnings
- Income may fluctuate dramatically from day to day or month to month
- Future earnings may be much lower (or zero)

**Not Typical:**
- Most members earn nothing or significantly less than amounts shown
- Earnings screenshots are selected for impact and inspiration
- They represent exceptional results, not expected outcomes

### 7.2 Time Investment Not Shown

**Testimonials rarely disclose:**
- How many hours per day or week were invested
- How long it took to achieve results (weeks, months, years)
- How much was earned per hour of work (effective hourly rate)
- Opportunity cost of time spent vs. other income opportunities

**A $10,000/month screenshot** may represent:
- 80 hours per week of work (equivalent to $11.50/hour)
- 2 years of unprofitable effort before seeing results
- Hundreds of hours of content creation and relationship building

**Always consider time investment when evaluating earnings claims.**

### 7.3 Prior Experience & Resources

Many high-earning testimonials come from individuals with:
- Extensive prior e-commerce experience
- Existing social media following or audience
- Professional video production skills
- Significant financial resources for advertising or inventory
- Business or marketing expertise from previous ventures

**These advantages are often not disclosed in testimonials** and significantly affect results.

## 10. NOT TYPICAL OR AVERAGE RESULTS

### 10.1 FTC Requirements

The Federal Trade Commission requires honest advertising and disclosure of typical results.

**TYPICAL RESULT DISCLOSURE:**

**$0 - Most people who purchase educational programs or online courses earn nothing because they do not implement the teachings, take sustained action, or persist through challenges.**

This is true for:
- Our Program specifically
- Online education and training generally
- E-commerce and affiliate marketing industries

### 10.2 Why Most People Earn Nothing

**Common reasons for zero earnings:**
- Never start implementing (consume content but don't take action)
- Give up after initial challenges or setbacks
- Lack of time, resources, or commitment
- Inconsistent effort or sporadic action
- Do not follow through on teachings
- External circumstances prevent participation
- TikTok Shop application denied or account issues
- Competition, market changes, or platform updates

**Testimonials showcase the small percentage of members who succeeded** despite these common challenges.

### 10.3 Survivorship Bias

**Testimonials suffer from "survivorship bias":**
- We only hear from members who succeeded (survivors)
- We don't display experiences from members who failed (non-survivors)
- This creates a distorted view of typical outcomes
- It overrepresents success and underrepresents failure

**For every success story shown, there may be dozens or hundreds of members who earned nothing.**
